Pencil

Where I feel most at home!
Whether a thoughtless doodle or a detailed realistic portrait, the simplicity of pencil has always managed to hold my attention and focus over any other medium.

Paint

My go to for maximum effect!
Paint can be the most frustrating medium to work with but it has also yielded some of my most vivid and impactful works so it's hard to stay angry at it for too long.

Digital

The newest addition to my repertoire!
Starting merely as a different way to get similar results, digital art quickly turned down the path of design, leading to creating website and company logos, digital illustrations, tattoo designs and more.
